Business Professionals of America hosts a multitude of leadership conferences each year where members may choose to participate in competitions and events and the regional, state, and national levels. Both team and individual events are available in a wide range of different subjects from web development and prepared speeches to financial analysis. Below is a list of different competitions that Business Professionals of America hosts. Team and individual events will be differentiated, and some events are only available to high school students while others are only available to people enrolled in a postsecondary institution.
